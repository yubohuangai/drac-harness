# Digital Research Alliance of Canada

Canada's national digital research infrastructure organization, formed from the merger of Compute Canada, CANARIE, and the Canadian Research Data Centre Network (CRDCN). Provides free HPC computing, cloud, and storage to Canadian researchers.

- Website: https://alliancecan.ca
- Documentation: https://docs.alliancecan.ca/wiki/Technical_documentation
- Account portal: https://ccdb.alliancecan.ca
- Support: support@tech.alliancecan.ca

Formerly known as **Compute Canada** — older documentation, command paths, and cvmfs mounts (`/cvmfs/soft.computecanada.ca/`) still use that name. Accounts and credentials are the same system. Some users and labs call it "DRAC" (Digital Research Alliance of Canada); the Alliance markets itself as "the Alliance."

## Active clusters (2026-05)

- **Narval** — A100-40G, established
- **Rorqual** — H100-80G, replaced Beluga (2026-Q2)
- **Fir** — H100-80G, replaced Cedar (2025-Q3)
- **Nibi** — H100-80G, replaced Graham (2025-Q3)
- **Trillium** — H100-80G + CPU, replaced Niagara/Mist (2025-Q3)
- **Arbutus** — Cloud (OpenStack)
